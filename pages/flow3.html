This flow was based on Adobe Illustrator, user test as card soring and the gangster test, knowledge about colors, typography, audience (målgruppe??), personas, information architecture and qualitative and quantitative data collection.
I started out by finding the audience so I could make the personas. You can find them here.

Audience segmentation and personas
I started out by finding the audience by doing geographical, demographic, psychographic, behavioral and technological segmentation, in order to make the personas. You can see the two personas here.


Information architecture
I made information architecture consisting of brainstorming, wireframes, sitemap and flowchart and then I made user tests so as card sorting on two persons. The two users did the test very differently and I chose to go with person number two because her solution matched my ideas the most.

I picked the blue color because it stands for credibility and commitment and addresses equally for men and woman(http://www.farvernesbetydning.dk/oversigt-over-farver-og-farvesymbolik/)
