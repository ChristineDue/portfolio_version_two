Adobe XD is an effective way to see process. It is a tool capable of producing a quick tempplate of how you what want your final project to look like.
I started out with a little research and found out that i would use the method "mobile first". Mobile first means that most things are being created with mobile in mind. Mobile devices are our way to stay connected and to communicate.


Because of the "mobile first"-term, I used UI kits to make the prototype more authentic.
Part of the prototyping is also usability-test. By testing the prototype on friends and family I found out what worked well and what didn't.
