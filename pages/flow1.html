Mobile first
I started researching and found out that I would use the method Mobile First. Mobile First is a term used to describe that most things are being created with mobile in mind. Mobile devices are the modern way to stay connected and to communicate.



UI kits
Because of the Mobile First-term, I used UI kits to make the prototype more authentic. Part of the prototyping is also usability-test. By testing the prototype on friends and family I found out what worked well and what didn't.

Adobe XD
Adobe XD is an effective way to see progress. It is a tool capable of producing a quick prototype of your project. When I started making the prototype I created a template with the basic layout that should be on all the pages. I added the burger menu and the search button as an icon in XD which made it easier to add on the different pages. At the picture on the left you can see my final prototype and how the different pages are linked to one another.

Feedback:
The feedback on this flow was, among other things, that I should have made a website instead because the company who would hire me as an intern would see my portfolio on a computer.
