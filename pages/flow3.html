Audience segmentation and personas
I started out by finding the audience by doing geographical, demographic, psychographic, behavioral and technological segmentation, in order to make the personas. You can see the two personas here.

Information architecture
I made information architecture consisting of brainstorming, wireframes, sitemap and flowchart and then I made user tests so as card sorting on two persons. The two users did the test very differently and I chose to go with person number two because her solution matched my ideas the most.

Icons
I made the icons for the app in illustrator. You can see my ordering of layers in illustrator and see the icons in my Creative Cloud library.

The first prototype
Afterwards, I created the first prototype in XD. The pictures are found at Unsplash.com and on google by searching after pictures which “may be used with modification”. I tested the app on two persons and did the thinking out loud test. I found out that I had to change the placement of where you can change the sport and that I should add a log out button on the profile page.

Colors
I picked the blue color because it symbolizes credibility and commitment and addresses equally to men and woman (source).


Feedback
The feedback at this flow was, that the lines in my icons were too thick and therefore they coalesce.

The second prototype
Therefore, I changed the icons with lines that coalesce. Thick lines were made thinner and the space between the head and the body on the profile icon were made bigger. I also added the suggestions from the user tests. You can see the changes in the pictures below.
